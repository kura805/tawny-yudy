Now let's put words together to make sentences! It's like LEGO but with words! 🧱

🧠 SECRET FORMULA:

Subject + Verb + Object

It's that simple! Just like English but with more flair! 💃

Spanish English Notes

😄 FUN FACT: Spanish people end sentences with the "upside down" question mark (¿) at the beginning and a regular one at the end. It's like your sentence is doing a somersault! 🤸
